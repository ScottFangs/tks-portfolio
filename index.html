About Me

ThatKidScott is a Jamaican-born rapper known for his dynamic presence in the music world, particularly on platforms like YouTube and Spotify. Drawing inspiration from alt/aesthetic rappers, rockstars and even emos he blends elements of hip-hop, nerdcore, aesthetic and meme music to create a unique and engaging sound. His music often combines punchy, high-energy verses with witty references to pop culture, appealing to a wide audience. Whether he's spitting bars with aggressive intensity or crafting more laid-back, romantic tracks, his versatility as an artist is a hallmark of his style.

His ability to switch from rapid-fire, S-tier lyrical delivery to smoother, more melodic flows sets him apart in the genre. This range allows ThatKidScott to experiment with different musical moods and themes, appealing to both hardcore rap fans and those looking for something lighter and more melodic. From banger tracks full of clever wordplay to heartfelt songs with emotional depth, his music reflects his diverse influences and his passion for creating songs that resonate with a wide variety of listeners. With a growing fan base, ThatKidScott is quickly becoming a prominent figure in the music scene, known for both his technical skill and his creative range.
